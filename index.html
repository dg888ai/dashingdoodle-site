FEATURES

🌍

9 Unique Stages

Journey from city streets through countryside, summer fields, mountain peaks, and winter wonderlands. Each stage has multi-layer parallax scrolling.

🐕

11+ Playable Breeds

Start with 3 free breeds and unlock more with bones. Each breed has 10 unique animations — run, jump, bark, sleep, and more!

⚡

Power-ups & Treats

Collect bones, drumsticks, golden bones, tennis balls, and the legendary Steak Mega Treat — ZOOMIES!!!

🔄

Lap System

Complete all 9 stages and loop back for another lap. How many laps can your doodle dash?

🎨

Retro Pixel Art

Beautiful 8-bit pixel art with bright, cheerful visuals. Pixel-perfect rendering at 640×360.

😴

Kid-Friendly

No violence — when your dog gets hit, they just take a nap! Designed for players of all ages.
