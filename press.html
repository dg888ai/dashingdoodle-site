DESCRIPTION

Dashing Doodle is an 8-bit pixel art endless runner where players dash through 9 beautifully crafted stages as one of 11 adorable dog breeds. From city streets to winter wonderlands, dodge obstacles, collect bones, and unlock new breeds and trail effects in the shop.

Designed for dog lovers of all ages, Dashing Doodle is completely kid-friendly — when your dog gets hit, they just take a nap! No violence, no personal data collection, fully COPPA compliant.

Built as a solo indie project in Godot 4.6, Dashing Doodle combines retro pixel art charm with modern mobile game design.

KEY FEATURES

  • 11 playable dog breeds — 3 free starters, 8 unlockable with in-game currency
  • 9 unique stages — city, nature, summer, and winter with multi-layer parallax
  • 7 trail effects — sparkles, hearts, fire, paw prints, star dust, neon, rainbow
  • Name your dog — each breed gets its own custom name
  • Power-ups — triple jump, magnet, drumstick speed boost, steak ZOOMIES
  • Progressive difficulty — speed ramps with each stage, birds get smarter each lap
  • Shop system — breeds, trails, and bone packs with in-game currency
  • Original music — 4 chiptune tracks with smooth crossfading
  • Kid-friendly — dogs "nap" on hit, no violence, G-rated ads, COPPA compliant
  • Free to play — cosmetic-only monetization, no pay-to-win

ABOUT THE DEVELOPER

KWX (KellenWorX) is an indie game studio based in San Diego, California. Dashing Doodle is the studio's debut title, inspired by the developer's own goldendoodle.

The game was built entirely by a solo developer using the open-source Godot 4.6 engine, with original pixel art and music.
